Level Up College

Diploma in Social and Development Studies

Level Up College is a community-based, non-profit higher education institution empowering youth from diverse backgrounds, especially those from marginalized communities and conflict-affected areas in Kayah (Karenni) State and the nearby region.

Vision/Mission

Vision

Empowered youth as quality human resources for the positive change of the community

Mission

Level Up Academy exists to create quality educational opportunities and provide education services through hands-on learnings and training by engaging cooperation, social justice, equity and diversity for all young people with various situations in Karenni State.

Our History

Level Up College was founded in 2012 by a team of inspired youth from Kayah/Karenni State under the guidance of Bishop Sotero Phamo. The college was established through the partnership with the Myanmar Community Academies Program (MCAP) under the Thabyay Foundation, with the recommendation and support of the Youth Life Formation Center (YLFC) and collaborative efforts from various community leaders.

Initially, Level Up College was launched as an extension program of YLFC in Loikaw and was named YLFC-Level Up Program. In 2013, it was officially registered as Level Up Academy with the Township Education Ministry Office.
