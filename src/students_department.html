Student Departments

At Level Up Academy, we believe that learning extends beyond the classroom. To help students develop valuable life skills and teamwork, we have established six residential committees within the hostel: the Energy Committee, Water Committee, Food Committee, Maintenance Committee, Gardening Committee, and Library Committee. Every hostel student is required to actively participate in one of these committees during their stay, contributing to the daily operations and overall well-being of the community.

Description of Each Department

Through participation in these committees, students gain practical experience in teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving, preparing them for future challenges beyond their academic years

Energy Committee

  • Responsible for monitoring solar batteries, operating the generator, and managing electricity usage in the school and hostel compound.
  • Ensures efficient and sufficient use of electricity, particularly during computer classes and office operations.

Water Committee

  • Manages the availability of clean drinking water and daily water use.
  • Focuses on ensuring a steady water supply while promoting water conservation within the school compound.

Food Committee

  • Plans nutritious and culturally diverse meals, ensuring a balanced diet for students.
  • Prepares breakfast daily, manages the food budget, oversees kitchen operations, and designs daily meal plans.

Maintenance Committee

  • Oversee the maintenance of school materials and equipment to prevent damage and loss.
  • Handles student requests for the use of school property and ensures its proper handling and care.

Gardening Committee

  • Provides fresh vegetables for the kitchen by cultivating crops using organic methods.
  • Works towards preserving local seeds and promoting sustainable agricultural practices.

Library Committee

  • Maintains and organizes the school library’s books and resources.
  • Encourages reading habits among students and enforces library policies and regulations.
Level Up College

Level Up College is a community-based, non-profit higher education institution empowering youth from diverse backgrounds, especially those from marginalized communities and conflict-affected areas in Kayah (Karenni) State and the nearby region..
