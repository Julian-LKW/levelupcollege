The Purpose of Student Council

The main purpose of having a student council at the academy is to strengthen the relationship between teachers and students. The student council plays an important role in encouraging students to take part in school activities, participate in community events, and represent the students' opinions to the management team. We hold elections for the student council every six months, allowing students to elect representatives who will serve and speak on their behalf. In this event, students who expressed interest in playing a role and leading as parliament members were selected by both students and teachers.
