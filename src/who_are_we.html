Level Up College

Level Up Colleges is a community-based, non-profit higher education institution empowering youth from diverse backgrounds, especially those from marginalized communities and conflict-affected areas in Kayah (Karenni) State and the nearby region.

The college offers a tailored curriculum for students, emphasizing social and development studies. The college is dedicated to promoting access to higher education, where contextualized academic excellence and holistic education are practiced through experiential learning, theoretical studies, and community engagement activities.

Founded in 2012, Level Up College believes in education as a catalyst for social change and values the collective wisdom of individuals, recognizing diverse knowledge and experiences. Level Up College is an educational institution that welcomes youth from all walks of life who aspire to become agents of positive change and contribute to the sustainable development of their communities. Level Up College aspires to be the top-choice institution for students seeking holistic, high-quality higher education in the region.

It is a worthwhile investment and contribution for philanthropists, foundations, and agencies seeking to support lasting and impactful social change for youth in Myanmar.

"Committed to equity and accessibility, the college provides full and partial scholarships, along with stipends, to financially disadvantaged students, ensuring that economic barriers do not hinder educational opportunities."

Our Philosophy

We believe in the power of education as a tool for social change, where collective wisdom is valued, individual knowledge and experiences are celebrated. We trust in learners’ potential to be positive change makers, emphasizing the uniqueness of their abilities. Educators play a role in nurturing these potentials within a healthy and safe environment through participatory, experiential, creative and reflective learning approaches.

Our Vision and Mission

Our Vision

Empowered youth as quality human resources for the positive change of the community

Our Mission

Level Up Academy exists to create quality educational opportunities and provide education services through hands-on learnings and training by engaging cooperation, social justice, equity and diversity for all young people with various situations in Karenni State.

Our Goals

1. To develop students attitude, knowledge, skill and network through providing quality education.
2. To increase students’ Community Spirit and the love of humanity and of nature through responding the needs of the rural and urban communities.
3. To promote diversity and equity through consultation and financial support.
4. To improve competence of youth for employment readiness and entrepreneur ventures.

Our Value

We work for those who we care about their needs aiming towards how we want to see them. All of our works are aiming towards our vision, strategic objectives and project goals, which are set because of our beliefs and values as follows.

Diversity

Equity

Quality

Sustainability
